Description

Tropeiro is a traditional Brazilian dish known for its hearty and flavorful ingredients. To prepare a delicious tropeiro, you will need ingredients such as beans, cassava flour, bacon, sausage, eggs, onions, garlic, collard greens, and farofa.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked black beans
  • 1 cup cassava flour
  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 pound (225g) smoked sausage, sliced
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 cups collard greens, thinly sliced
  • Farofa (toasted cassava flour), for serving
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pan, cook the chopped bacon until crispy. Remove the bacon from the pan and set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add the sliced sausage and cook until browned. Remove the sausage from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté the diced onions and minced garlic until translucent.
  4. Add the cooked black beans to the pan and stir well to combine with the onions and garlic. Cook for a few minutes until heated through.
  5. In a separate pan, fry the eggs to your preferred doneness.
  6. In another pan, sauté the collard greens until wilted.
  7. Add the cassava flour to the pan with the beans and mix well to incorporate. Cook for a few more minutes until the flour is toasted.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve the tropeiro in individual bowls, topping each portion with the crispy bacon, sliced sausage, fried eggs, sautéed collard greens, and a sprinkle of farofa.
  10. Enjoy your flavorful Brazilian tropeiro!
